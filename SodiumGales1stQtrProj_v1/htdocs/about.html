About Us

We are the leading university in the Philippines

Benford University Press advances knowledge and learning. The possibilities are boundless, from a little child reading their first words to a researcher pushing the boundaries of their field. We have been a haven for the curious, enthusiastic, and ambitious throughout our 500-year history, dating back to the early days of printing. We encourage innovative thinking and new ideas, and we provide both individuals and groups the chance to stand out. Our aim is to develop the best academic and educational resources and services and to make them accessible globally. This mission statement guides all we do. We share the University's strict standards, distinguishing characteristics, and conviction in the transformational power of research and education to spur advancement and realize human potential.
