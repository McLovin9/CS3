Our Courses

Courses We Offer

Benford offers more than 350 graduate courses across the disciplines, with courses opening in new areas every year. This includes taught courses and research courses available for full-time and part-time study, and at every level from diplomas to doctorates.

Taught Courses

•Master of Philosophy (Mphil)
•Master of Studies (MSt)
•Master of Science (MSc)
•Other master’s-level degrees (BCL, BPhil, EMBA, MBA, MFA, MJur, MPP, and MTh)
•Postgraduate Diploma (PGDip) and Postgraduate Certificate (PGCert and PGCE)

Research Courses

•Doctor of Philosophy (DPhil)
•Doctoral training programmes (CDTs, DTPs, and DEng)
•Master of Science (MSc)
•Combined Master of Science (MSc) and Doctor of Philosophy (DPhil)

Part-Time Courses

•Ancient History (DPhil)
•Classical Languages and Literature (DPhil)
•Criminology (DPhil)
•Economics (DPhil)
•Politics (DPhil)
Sociology (MSc).

World Class Library

Containing a wide collection of informative and helpful books. The Library can be found at the second floor in any Benford University Campus. More than the book services, the Library offers other helpful services through their Internet/Electronic Resources Station, Charging Station, Leisure Section, Reading Corner, etc.

Benford Community Park

Benford Community Park has two playgrounds: one smaller playground designed for ages 2-5 and a larger playground designed for ages 5-12. Both playgrounds have safety surfacing. The larger playground has a canopy shelter with several picnic tables. Located between the two playgrounds are basketball courts, volleyball court, shuffleboard, baseball/softball diamonds and open green space.

Cafeteria/Canteen

The cafeteria/canteen can be found in the 1st floor of the School, beside the student groups' tambayans. This serves meals, snacks, beverages, and treats from breakfast time until an early dinner.
